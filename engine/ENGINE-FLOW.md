# LOGOS Engine Flow

LOGOS is a pipeline for transforming raw human observation into tested meaning.

---

## Canonical Flow

```text
Observation
→ Human Truth
→ Human Contradiction
→ Belief Shift
→ Meaning Atom
→ Story Pattern
→ Emotional Result
→ Script
→ Content
→ Experiment
→ Metrics
→ Learning
→ Law Candidate
→ LOGOS Law
```

---

## Stage 1 — Observation

A raw noticing from real life.

Example:

> Women invest heavily into visible beauty routines but may postpone invisible health routines.

---

## Stage 2 — Human Truth

A general human pattern extracted from the observation.

Example:

> People naturally care more about what they see every day.

---

## Stage 3 — Human Contradiction

A tension between self-image and behavior.

Example:

> I believe I care for myself, but I ignore parts of myself that are not visible.

---

## Stage 4 — Belief Shift

The old belief is transformed into a new belief.

Example:

```text
Old Belief:
Self-care is mostly visible care.

New Belief:
True self-care does not end at the surface.
```

---

## Stage 5 — Meaning Atom

A compact carrier of the new belief.

Example:

> True care for yourself does not end at the surface.

---

## Stage 6 — Story Pattern

A reusable narrative container.

Example:

```text
Mirror → visible routine → hidden value → reframe → calm action
```

---

## Stage 7 — Emotional Result

The feeling the audience should leave with.

Example:

> I am not being frightened. I am being invited to respect myself more fully.

---

## Stage 8 — Script

A platform-specific expression of the Meaning Atom.

For DeflaGyn runtime, avatar speech is Ukrainian.

---

## Stage 9 — Experiment

A published test with measurable outcomes.

---

## Stage 10 — Learning

Interpretation of results.

Important questions:

- Did people watch?
- Did they save?
- Did they share?
- Did they quote the idea?
- Did comments show belief movement?

---

## Stage 11 — Law Candidate

A repeated pattern that may become a LOGOS Law.

---

## Stage 12 — LOGOS Law

A validated principle of meaning and behavior.
