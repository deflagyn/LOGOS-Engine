It is not a real runtime campaign.

Total score:


Interpretation:

```text
Strong enough to continue testing, but not yet enough for a law candidate.
```

---

## Learning

The scoring model works for a basic sample because it separates attention, retention, action, meaning and learning.

This prevents a high view count from being treated as proof by itself.
