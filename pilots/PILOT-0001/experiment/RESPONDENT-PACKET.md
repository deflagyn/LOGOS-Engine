# PILOT-0001 Respondent Packet

Status: ready_for_real_respondent_use

---

## Before You Read

You will read a short draft scene.

There are no right answers.

Please describe what you actually notice, including anything that feels unclear, forced, transactional or uncomfortable.

Do not include personal contact information in your answers.

---

## Use This Handout

Give the respondent:

```text
pilots/PILOT-0001/experiment/RESPONDENT-HANDOUT.md
```

The handout contains only the respondent-facing scene and questions.

Do not give the respondent source-object notes, traceability IDs or LOGOS internal sections before they answer.

---

## Questions

1. Что, по вашему ощущению, изменилось у героя после безопасного пространства?

2. Выглядит ли отдача в финале добровольной или обязанной? Почему?

3. Есть ли в сцене ощущение давления, долга или манипуляции? Где именно?

4. Как вы понимаете метафору расширения возможностей в этой сцене?

---

## Optional Short Scores

Score each from `0` to `5`.

```text
0 = absent or contradicted
1 = barely present
2 = weak / unclear
3 = present but not strong
4 = clear
5 = very clear
```

Signals:

```text
voluntary_safety
recovery_as_resource
non_coercive_return
boundary_awareness
metaphorical_expansion
```

---

## Important Negative Signals

Please say explicitly if the scene feels like:

```text
woman owes safety
man owes return
relationship as transaction
resource sharing as payment
expansion as domination
safety as manipulation
```

Negative readings are useful evidence.
