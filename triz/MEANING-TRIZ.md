# Meaning TRIZ

## Thesis

Classical TRIZ helps resolve contradictions in technical systems.

Meaning TRIZ adapts this logic to human, cultural and narrative systems.

Where technical TRIZ asks:

> What contradiction prevents the system from evolving?

Meaning TRIZ asks:

> What human contradiction prevents a person from accepting a new belief, habit or product as meaningful?

## Human Contradiction

A human contradiction appears when a person holds two incompatible realities.

Example:

```text
I believe I care for myself.
But I ignore the invisible parts of my health.
```

The goal is not to shame the person.

The goal is to help them resolve the contradiction through a new belief.

---

## Ideal Meaning Result

The Ideal Meaning Result occurs when the audience arrives at the desired thought as if it were their own discovery.

This is how strong parables work.

This is also how strong brand narratives work.

The best meaning does not feel imposed.

It feels recognized.

---

## Meaning Resources

Meaning resources are already present inside the audience.

Examples:

- love;
- responsibility;
- desire for calm;
- desire to be a good mother;
- self-respect;
- desire for dignity;
- fear of regret;
- wish to live freely;
- habit of visible self-care.

LOGOS does not invent these resources. It activates and reorders them.

## First Psychological TRIZ Principles

### 01. Divide the concept

Split one familiar concept into two levels.

Example:

```text
Beauty → visible beauty / invisible value
Self-care → surface care / complete care
```

### 02. Invert the causal direction

Reverse what the audience assumes causes what.

Example:

```text
Not: beauty creates calm.
But: calm allows beauty to be enjoyed.
```

### 03. Remove the product from the center

The product becomes stronger when it stops being the hero.

### 04. Use hidden resources

Use values the audience already has.

Example:

A woman already values care, beauty, family and calm. LOGOS connects women's health to these existing resources.

### 05. Turn information into identity

Facts explain. Identity moves.

Example:

```text
Not: you should do this.
But: this is what a woman who cares for herself completely does.
```

### 06. Resolve anxiety through reasonable action

Do not promise total safety. Offer a truthful path from helpless worry to reasonable care.
